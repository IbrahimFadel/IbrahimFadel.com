UCC Book Exchange

Still in Development

The UCC Book Exchange was my IB Personal Project. It is a website that helps connect students at my high school who want to sell/buy textbooks. It's always a struggle finding someone who needs your book, or someone who has the one you want. Especially if you don't know people in different grades. This website allows you to browse textbooks people have listed, message the owner and place a bid. It makes buying and selling your textbooks super simple.
