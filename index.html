Portfolio

Sandscript

tl;dr Sandscript is a small compiler i'm making with C++ & LLVM. It's nowhere near done or even at a state resembling a real language -- but every time i rewrite it I get a bit better, and looks a bit cleaner.

In Development

SIRC

tl;dr Simple IRC -- this project was my attempt at creating a tiny IRC client in Typescript. It's not nearly as feature complete as most IRC clients(all you can do is join channels, and message people) but it was mostly just to learn about implementing a protocol like IRC.

Albumify

tl;dr After switching from Itunes to Spotify I was upset that spotify didn't have an simplified view where you can only see album art and play status. I made a little program that runs alongside Spotify and does just that with C++ & SFML.

Itunes to Spotify

tl;dr Python script that moves itunes playlists to spotify. Not close to perfect -- but I was able to transfer roughly 95% of my songs with pretty good accuracy. Overall, much easier than doing it manually(and not limited to 200 songs like some services I found). Looking back though, it's way too verbose.

RorkeJS

tl;dr RorkeJS is a little game library I made with PixiJS(WebGL renderer). It let's you load in sprites very easily, make animations, and handle collisions. It's physics is bare-bones but enough to make a small game.

mywm

tl;dr Teensy X11 window manager. Nothing really too interesting to see in this project -- just me trying to get a bare-bones window manager working. It's still in very early stages of development.
